Interior Panels

With LedGo Interior Panels you create a wall, floor or ceiling comprised of LED-panels on which all possible types of moving images, films or animations can be shown. In this way any interior can be changed into an experience. The unique LED panels have been developed by LedGo specialists.

With LedGo Interior Panels ceilings, floors and walls are no longer static surfaces, but the media displays on which experiences are shown. The possibilities are endless; the only limit is your own perception.

LedGo Interior Panels are ideal for digital signage and for providing information in every conceivable space. Moving images provide an unprecedented experience, but also still images create atmosphere in the room.

Even when the LED panels are turned off they still have a decorative appearance, because the front plate can be produced in countless designs and colours. A front made of wood or a mirror is also possible. In addition, the LED panels can be used as a source of light.

The technically, extremely advanced Interior Panels have been developed by the Dutch Company, LedGo. LedGo is a leading player in the field of innovative LED technology.
Design and technology going hand in hand.

Due to the low weight and reduced power requirements LedGo Interior Panels can easily be placed against a wall, in a floor, or in a (system) ceiling. No major structural adjustments are necessary, which is still required for standard LED panels. Installation is very simple with the assistance of accessories such as hanging bars, controllers and content which can be supplied by LedGo. Temporary rental of LedGo Interior Panels is also possible.
